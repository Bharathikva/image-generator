Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the AI Image Generator.

General

What is the AI Image Generator?

The AI Image Generator is a tool that transforms text descriptions into stunning visuals using advanced artificial intelligence.

How does it work?

Simply enter a prompt describing your idea, and the AI processes it to generate an image based on your input. You can customize options like style, aspect ratio, and guidance scale for better results.

Is it free to use?

We offer a free trial with limited features. For full access, you can explore our subscription plans or pay-per-use options.

Usage

What kind of prompts can I use?

You can use detailed, descriptive text to specify your idea. For example, "a futuristic cityscape at sunset in a cyberpunk style" works well. Avoid overly generic or ambiguous prompts.

What is a negative prompt?

A negative prompt allows you to specify elements you want the AI to avoid in the generated image, such as certain colors, objects, or styles.

What is the guidance scale?

The guidance scale controls how closely the AI follows your prompt. Higher values mean stricter adherence to your input, while lower values allow for more creative freedom.

Technical Details

What are the supported aspect ratios?

We currently support square (1:1), landscape (16:9), and portrait (9:16) aspect ratios.

How long does it take to generate an image?

Most images are generated within 10-20 seconds, depending on the complexity of the prompt and server load.

What formats are the images available in?

Images are generated in high-quality PNG format, ensuring clarity and sharpness.

Troubleshooting

Why is my image not generating?

Ensure your prompt is detailed and descriptive. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or try again later during off-peak hours.

I see an error message. What should I do?

Error messages usually indicate a temporary issue. Note the error code and contact our support team for assistance if the issue continues.

Can I restore deleted images?

Unfortunately, deleted images cannot be restored. Make sure to download or save your images immediately after generation.
