How It Works

Learn how our AI Image Generator transforms your ideas into stunning visuals using advanced technology.

Overview

Our AI Image Generator uses the powerful Pix-Art-Sigma Model via the MonsterAPI platform to convert your text prompts into visually captivating images. The process is simple, efficient, and highly customizable, ensuring your ideas come to life just as you envision them.

Step-by-Step Process

1. Enter Your Prompt

Start by describing the image you want to generate. Be as detailed as possible to help the AI understand your vision. For example: "A futuristic cityscape at sunset in a cyberpunk style."

2. Choose Custom Options

Customize the output with options like aspect ratio, style, guidance scale, and negative prompts. These settings give you control over the final image.

3. Submit Your Prompt

Once you're satisfied with your inputs, hit the "Generate Image" button. The AI sends your prompt to the MonsterAPI, where it is processed using the Pix-Art-Sigma Model.

4. Fetch and View Results

After submitting, the API returns a process_id. Our system fetches the generated image using this ID and displays it in the output section for you to download or share.
